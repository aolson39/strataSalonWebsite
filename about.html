Our Story

Our passion and purpose is bringing
trend-facing, modern hair to Agoura Hills

Our founders grew up in Carlsbad, CA and were struck by the lack of modern salon offerings for the community upon their return - so they did something about it. Strata Salon opened its doors to fulfill an unmet desire for high quality, contemporary hair care in the Carlsbad Area.

Trained and apprenticed in Los Angeles, Chicago, and New York City, our founders envisioned Strata Salon to be a marriage of urban style and the SoCal vibe.
